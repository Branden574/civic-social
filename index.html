Core Features

Built different.
Because the stakes are different.

Every feature is designed to surface signal, reduce noise, and keep discourse grounded in fact.

◈

Context Panels

Context that follows the conversation. Every post can surface sources, summaries, and linked legislation automatically.

⊙

Live Legislation Tracker

Legislation updates, not headlines. Track bill status, amendments, votes, and committee actions in real time.

◎

Transparent Feeds

Feeds you can understand. Choose chronological or ranked — and see exactly why a post appears in your feed.

◐

Civility Controls

Tools that keep discourse human. Conversation health scores, auto-de-escalation nudges, and cooling-off features.

✦

Verified Profiles

A trust layer that means something. Human verification tied to professional credentials, not just payment.

◧

Community Notes

Citation-based, community-driven corrections. Neutral, source-first, and visible to all — not buried.

How It Works

Three steps.
Infinite clarity.

01

Pick topics you care about

Select policy areas, committees, and communities. Your feed builds itself around what matters to you — not what drives engagement.

02

Follow people & communities

Follow verified experts, journalists, legislators, and citizens. Community circles let you filter signal from noise.

03

Track posts & policy updates

One feed. Posts, bill alerts, context panels, and community notes — unified and chronologically honest.

Trust & Safety

Neutral by design.
Safe by default.

We built the principles before we built the product. That's not a tagline — it's the architecture.

◎

Neutral by Design

No algorithmic amplification of outrage. No partisan weighting. Feed ranking is documented and auditable.

◈

Transparent Ranking

Every ranking signal is published. You choose what drives your feed — not a black box optimizing for time-on-site.

◐

Anti-Harassment

Proactive protections, not reactive bans. Conversation health monitoring and tiered reporting tools.

⊙

Source-First Info

Primary sources surface before opinions. Context panels link to the original document, not a summary of a summary.
